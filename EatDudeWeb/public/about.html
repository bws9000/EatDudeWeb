Welcome to Eat Dude mobile restaurant menus. Our goal is to make the accessibility of restaurant menus more convenient. Our menu management application is a great way for restaurant owners to maintain their menus and give "up to the minute" updates to mobile devices.

I got tired of sorting through my stack of takeout menus one day and just wanted them to be in one place I could access quickly and conveniently. I also wanted my "stack of menus" when I was away from home. I just wanted an easy way to store them on my phone, find what I wanted, click a button and call when I was ready to order.

If your favorite restaurant isn't listed contact us with the details. If you want to add a menu to the system or received an invite code to manage a current menu sign-in with your free google account.
